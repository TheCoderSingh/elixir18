ABOUT ELIXIR

Elixir is a technical, entrepreneurial and cultural festival being held for the first time in Apeejay College of Fine Arts. Elixir will provide a platform to the students all over the country to showcase their knack and flair through various cultural, entrepreneurial, technical, literary and quizzing competitions. Elixir will swank of performance from the stalwarts of the entertainment industry to perform and enthral the throng.

Elixir aims to ingrain a culture of passion and industriousness through the events that are organised at the eccentric level. The current version of the Elixir is aiming for a participation that echoes with vigour and zeal bundled together in an unparalleled extravaganza.

The colossal event in the city that is proudly bestowed by Apeejay College of Fine Arts and is organised by the students of the college aims to become the annual cultural - technical festival of Apeejay College of Fine Arts and earn a reputation that will speak for itself.

With an ‘A’ grade by NAAC to its credit, the college is recognized today as a trail-blazer as well as a symbol of personalized, student-centric quality education of global dimensions. Achieving milestones and creating history, Apeejay has striven to be ahead of others in Academics as well as co-curricular activities.

The college focuses on the modern-day academic & professional qualifications needed to give students a cutting edge over others. Apeejay’s courses are specially designed, structured and modified from time to time to meet the intense pressure & competitions at all levels and also to maximize employment opportunities at the global level.
